Paola, the manager, helped send out the machines to buyers for the product and make sure they’re satisfied with the machine. Brian, the developer, help create the concept of how the machine would work for everyday purpose. Finally we have Tenzin, the designer, he created the layout of how the machine would work to attract people in today’s society.
