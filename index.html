We know that not everyone has time to do their daily tasks. Our product, the Folding Machine 5000, is staple in today’s society. It allows people to have their clothes folded for them so that they can use their time elsewhere. Our mission statement is to help people across the world use their time on other things that can help them in life. They shouldn’t have to put their time into folding clothes. After all, time is money.
